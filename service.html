Outreach

I currently lead the new public outreach initiative of the Helsinki-Aalto Center for Information Security (HAIC). In this capacity, I established HAIC Talks, a new series of public lectures on topics related to information security, given by world experts but aimed at a general audience. Videos of past talks and details of upcoming events are published at: haic.aalto.fi/talks

During my DPhil I was a STEM Ambassador with Science Oxford. I am currently participating in the Scientists in Schools programme at Aalto University.
