Outreach

As Deputy Director of the Helsinki-Aalto Center for Information Security (HAIC), I established HAIC Talks, a new series of public lectures on topics related to information security, given by world experts but aimed at a general audience. Videos of past talks and details of upcoming events are published at: haic.aalto.fi/talks
